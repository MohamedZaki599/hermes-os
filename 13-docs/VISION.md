# Executive Summary

Hermes OS is not another AI assistant.

Hermes OS is an AI-powered Operating System designed to become the second brain, operations manager, technical advisor, business strategist, and execution engine for a solo entrepreneur.

Its purpose is not to answer questions.

Its purpose is to help build businesses.

Every project, every client, every decision, every document, every workflow, and every lesson learned should become part of Hermes' long-term knowledge.

Hermes grows together with its owner.

---

# Why Hermes Exists

Modern AI tools are powerful.

However...

They forget.

They lose context.

They cannot build long-term operational knowledge.

Every conversation starts almost from zero.

A solo entrepreneur constantly switches between:

ChatGPT

Gemini

GitHub

Canva

VS Code

Documents

Ideas

Projects

Clients

Marketing

Invoices

Knowledge

Nothing connects everything together.

Hermes exists to become the operating system that connects all of them.

---

# The Problem

Current AI assistants are session-based.

Business is memory-based.

Current AI assistants generate.

Businesses execute.

Current AI assistants provide answers.

Businesses require decisions.

Hermes bridges this gap.

---

# Mission

Build an operating system that allows one person to operate with the efficiency of a complete company.

---

# Vision

To become the central operating system for every solo entrepreneur who wants to build products, manage clients, automate operations, and continuously improve through accumulated knowledge.

# Core Principles

## Principle 1

The best answer comes from understanding the user's history, projects, and goals.

---

## Principle 2

Knowledge compounds.

Every completed project should make Hermes smarter.

Nothing should be forgotten.

---

## Principle 3

Execution wins.

Hermes is designed to finish work, not just discuss work.

---

## Principle 4

One source of truth.

Projects, documents, workflows, prompts, memories, and decisions must live inside Hermes.

---

## Principle 5

Models change.
